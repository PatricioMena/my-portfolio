Our Process

From concept to launch 🚀

Crafting your vision: Our step-by-step journey

step 1

Discovery

Setting goals and purpose. Creating a plan to outline the project's needs and objectives

  • Examine competitors and identify target audience

  • Define technical requirements and desired features

  • Establish communication channels

  • Define the scope, timeline, and budget

step 2

Design

Crafting the visual and functional components, collaborating with you to reflect your brand identity

  • Wireframe to test ideas quickly

  • Full-design mockups

  • Visual content creation

  • Review and feedback

step 03

Code / Development

Now your project is taking shape! In this phase, we write the code to implement the design and features from the previous phases

  • Front-end and back-end development

  • Performance optimization

  • Social media integration

  • Analytics tools installation

step 04

Testing and Quality Assurance

It's time to guarantee that your project performs as expected. We will ensure your project performs to your expectations

  • Features and functionality

  • Responsiveness on multiple devices

  • Broken links and design inconsistences

  • Cross browser compatibility

  • Load times and performance

step 05

Deployment

Once all testing is complete and you're satisfied with the results, it's time to launch and bring your website or app to life!

  • Select the perfect domain name

  • SSL certification installation

  • Publish to the web!

  • Run tests to ensure everything is working as planned

step 06

Maintenance

Now that your project is live, let's make sure it stays secure, functional, and up-to-date! After launch we will keep monitoring your project for 30 days so we won't leave you hanging

  • Monitor performance and traffic

  • Provide the latest improvements for optimal performance

  • Security verification

  • Ensure backups to protect your data
